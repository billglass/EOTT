About Us

Company Overview

Eye of the Tiger, LLC is a Primary Care Optometric Practice. We aim to provide every patient with a comprehensive eye exam, which would not only find and correct current vision problems, but will also allow us to prevent future ones.

We provide comprehensive eye exam for adults and children. Our patients also have the option of being fitted for the latest technology in contact lenses. Both doctors are fully trained in the fitting of spherical, toric, multifocal, gas permeable and specialty contact lenses.  Our practice screens every patient for glaucoma, macular degeneration, and other vision threatening disorders. If detected, these disorders will be followed and managed with additional state of the art equipment.

Our goal is to provide our patients with the option of choosing high quality, fashionable, and affordable frames and lenses. Our experienced and friendly Opticians will be sure to meet and exceed your expectations by offering great quality product with speedy turn around time. We strive to be a practice that our patients will come back to year after year based on our overall experience.

 

Meet the Doctors of Optometry

Pooja Sheth, OD

Dr. Pooja Sheth (27OA00612600) received her Bachelor of Arts in Biology from the University of Texas in 2003.  She continued her education at the University of Houston College of Optometry, where she graduated in 2007, with honors. She completed her externship at the Veterans Affairs Hospital in Lyons, NJ.  Professional experience includes ocular surgery co-management, specialty contact lens fitting, diagnosis and management of ocular disease, ocular trauma and primary care optometry.  She speaks Gujarati, Hindi, and Spanish. In her spare time, she enjoys traveling, reading, and her occasional fix of celebrity gossip and reality television.

 

Priyam Patel, OD

Dr. Priyam Patel (27OA00633000) is an optometrist specializing in comprehensive eye care, treatment and diagnoses of the eye diseases and in contact lenses fittings. She also has extensive experience in pre and post-operative management care for LASIK, cataracts, and laser for glaucoma and diabetic eye disease. Dr. Patel received her doctor of Optometry from Pennsylvania College of Optometry at Salus University and received her bachelor’s degree in Biology from Wilkes University. Dr. Patel finished her clinical internship training at The Eye Institute in Philadelphia, PA. She earned clinical honors through out her internships. Dr. Patel is an active member of the American Optometric Association (AOA) and New Jersey Society of Optometric Physician (NJSOP).
